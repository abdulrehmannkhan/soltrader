Tokenomics that works.
Every small detail is brainstormed, how small it may seem.
Low Tax Fees
Minimal tax fees on buys and sells, ensuring that users retain a significant portion of their assets during trades. This feature fosters an environment where participants benefit from reduced overheads.
Contract Renounced
The SOLTrader contract is renounced, ensuring a transparent and decentralized environment. By renouncing ownership, we underscore our commitment to community governance and trust.
Tax Redistribution
Taxes collected from transactions are redistributed among holders, rewarding participation and commitment within the SOLTrader community. This redistribution mechanism amplifies the benefits for long-term supporters.
Automated Buyback Mechanism
SOLTrader employs an automated buyback mechanism, utilizing a portion of transaction taxes to repurchase tokens from the market. This strategic process bolsters liquidity and supports token value, creating a continuous cycle that benefits holders.
